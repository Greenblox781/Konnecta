Our Story

Konnecta was born from a simple conversation between two entrepreneurs who shared a common frustration: talented small service providers and local businesses were struggling to connect with customers who desperately needed their services.
In early 2025, founders Xolani Mgidi and Nicholas Daniel met at a New Life Church community event called Young Adults. Xolani had been observing a gap in the market—skilled professionals and small business owners lacked an effective platform to showcase their services and reach their ideal customers. When he learned that Nicholas was studying web development, he knew they had found the perfect partnership to bring this vision to life. What started as an idea quickly became a passion project. Nicholas dove headfirst into building the platform, often teaching himself advanced concepts when the pace of traditional learning couldn't keep up with his ambitious ideas. This hands-on approach meant Konnecta was built by someone who truly understood both the technical challenges and the real-world needs of small businesses.
Our mission is simple: to be the bridge that connects talented service providers and entrepreneurs with the customers who need them most. We believe every skilled professional deserves the opportunity to grow their business, and every customer deserves easy access to quality local services
