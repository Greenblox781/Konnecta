How It works

For Service Seekers

1

Browse Available Providers

  • Search by category, location, price
  • View provider profiles and offerings
  • Read reviews and ratings

2

Compare & Choose

  • Compare different providers
  • Check availability and pricing
  • Review portfolios/past work

3

Book & Connect

  • Contact your chosen provider
  • Discuss details and finalize terms
  • Schedule the service

4

Get It Done

  • Provider completes the work
  • Pay through the platform
  • Leave a review

For Service Providers

1

Create Your Service Listing

  • Post what services you offer
  • Set your rates and availability
  • Upload photos/portfolio

2

Get Discovered

  • Customers browse and find your listing
  • Receive inquiries and booking requests
  • Respond to potential clients

3

Deliver Your Service

  • Complete the work for your client
  • Get paid through the platform
  • Build your reputation with reviews
